Laura has been in the SBK scene for nearly 20 years and developed a real passion for fusing styles, such as salsa, with her other loves, such as hip hop or contemporary. Highlights of her career have included choreographing with New York Fashion Week and touring as a dancer with an alternative fashion campaign.
