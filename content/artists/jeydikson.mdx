Born in Sao Tome, Jeydikson moved to Portugal when he was eight years old. He played third league football for a Portuguese team before discovering his passion for dance. He started dancing hip-hop and R\&B at school, and later developed other styles like breakdancing, house, Afro-beats, contemporary, and Kuduro. He discovered Kizomba at age 19 and fell in love with it
