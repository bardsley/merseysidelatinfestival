Moe Flex is a world-renowned MC, artist, choreographer, teacher, entertainer, and storyteller. Since 1998, Moe has built a stellar reputation for excellence in dance, mastering movement, musicality, and technique. His award-winning work as a teacher and choreographer has taken him across the globe, adapting his expertise to a wide array of dance styles.

Known for his professionalism, infectious fun, and meticulous attention to detail, Moe is a sought-after choreographer worldwide. He brings his authentic style to every event, creating a unique and engaging atmosphere, whether teaching or hosting.
